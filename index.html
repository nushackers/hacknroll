Hack&Roll 2014


25th - 26th January.

About

Hack&Roll is an annual 24-hour Hackathon organized by NUS Hackers, a student-run organization committed to spreading hacker culture. Designed to grow and strengthen the local hacking community, Hack&Roll 2014 will be an open platform for hackers to gather, collaborate, and build new things. In the spirit of hacking, we not only celebrate the most useful products, but also reward projects for factors such as overall coolness and "awesome but useless".

Hack&Roll 2013 saw about 80 students attending and building projects ranging from a WiFi-based room headcount system to a remote-controlled car driven by facial tracking. Prizes included a weeklong all-expense-paid trip to Silicon Valley, smartphones, quadricopters, and mini-computers.

Hack&Roll 2014 will be held over 25th and 26th January, with over 100 students from across Singapore expected to attend. Regardless of whether you're studying in university, junior college or polytechnic, we welcome you to join us for a weekend of hacking!

If you're interested in connecting with our participants, our sponsorship team would love to discuss possible methods of collaboration with you. Do contact us!

Rules

  1. This event is open only to:
    1. Students of all levels in any educational institution in Singapore (yes, exchange students count as well!)
    2. Anyone waiting entry into an educational institution - e.g. NSFs, PhD candidates, etc
  2. A team can have between 1 and 4 members.
  3. All teams need to stay for the duration of the event. We do, you know, recommend bringing sleeping bags. Teams that leave will be disqualified.
  4. Build something new. You may reuse code for specific components, but recycling an entire project is not OK.
  5. Projects are judged based on creativity, technical accomplishment, polish, and usefulness, in that order.
  6. Decisions made by the judges are final.
  7. The organizers reserve the right to change the above rules at any time.
