In-Kind sponsorship

  • Food

    To make sure everyone is well-fueled throughout the event, Hack&Roll caters meals for our participants. Over the duration of the event, we plan to provide 4 meals and a generous supply of snacks.

  • Coffee

    Hackers often consume caffeinated drinks to power them through the night, and we would like to keep them well–supplied. Of course, other beverages (including healthier options) are also welcome.

  • Fridge

    Having drinks is great. Having chilled drinks is even better. We would love to have (or simply borrow) a fridge to store beverages, and maybe even fruits or ice cream.

If you’ve got anything else you’d like sponsor, we’d be glad to discuss this as well - we’re open to your ideas!
