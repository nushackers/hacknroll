Food

To ensure everyone is well-fueled throughout the event, Hack&Roll provides meals for our participants. During the event, we plan to provide 4 meals and a generous supply of snacks.

Coffee

Hackathon participants often consume caffeinated drinks to power them through the night, and we would like to keep them well–supplied. Of course, other beverages (including healthier options) are also welcome.

Fridge

Having drinks is great. Having chilled drinks is even better. We would love to have (or simply borrow) a fridge to store beverages, ice cream, and fruits.
